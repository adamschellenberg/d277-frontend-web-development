Welcome to Arizona

Arizona is the 48th state (but #1 in humor, which we've scattered around this website), having gained statehood on February 14, 1912. We're the birthplace of popular bands such as Jimmy Eat World, Meat Puppets, Scary Kids Scaring Kids, Alice Cooper (the band) and Blessthefall. But you knew that, right?

Top 3 reasons to visit us, please:
  1. Grand Canyon
  2. Sedona
  3. Horseshoe Bend

Oh, and we have the Grand Canyon, too. That thing is pretty cool. I mean, who doesn't love a good canyon, amiright?

Uhhh, let's see here... There's other stuff, too. Y'know, like... uhh... The Arizona Coyotes? A hockey team in a desert state makes sense.

Okay, look. We might not be as cool as Alaska, or as green as Tennessee, but trust me, we're worth a visit. Click here to see all the cool stuff AZ has to offer!
